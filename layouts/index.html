About the Game

Zero Bugs Given is a tower defense game where no two runs are the same. Build your defenses on a procedurally generated hexagonal map, choose your strategy through a risk-reward system of bonuses and maluses, and face increasingly challenging waves of enemies.

Every decision matters: which turrets to place, which bonuses to pick, and which maluses to accept. Adapt your strategy on the fly as the map and enemy composition change with each playthrough.
